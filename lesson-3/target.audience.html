Audience Description

The target audience is between the ages of 15-70. They will access Up To Date Weather mainly from smart phones and at times from tablets, laptops, or desktop computers. The audience would include families in the community looking to attend an outdoor community event. Fatherss and sons taking a weekend fishing trip. Out of state visitors planning a snowmobile trip on the pristine mountain trails.

Personas

Mick

He is an HVAC repairman who works hard and plays hard. He is an outdoor enthusiast. He is 45 years old. He is married with two boys. He values spending time with his family camping, fishing, hiking and cross country skiing. It is important for him to know what to expect from the weather so that he can be prepared. He would also expect an accurate and current angler report so that he can teach his sons how to fish in the best possible spots.

Napoleon

He is a 15 year old boy scout who lives in Preston, Idaho. He serves, in his moon boots, as the senior patrol leader of his troop. He is a freshman in high school and is a member of his school cross country team. He enjoys camping, hiking, fishing, feeding Tina the llama and attending community events with his family. As a boy scout he lives by the motto 'be prepared'. When planning an event with his troop he wants an accurate forecast. To get the forecast he would visit uptodateweather.com using his smart phone.

Scenarios

My boy scout troop is planning a campout for this weekend, what will the weather be like?

I have a free weekend to go fishing, I wonder what the fishing is like at Glendale Resavior?

The winter carnaval in Soda Springs is this weekend. I wonder how warm I need to dress?

I wonder if it is going to rain on the rodeo this weekend in Preston?

I have a cross country meet this week. What kind of weather should I plan for?

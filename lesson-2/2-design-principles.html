Design Principles Document

Kimberly Uresti

Alignment

Kroger
www.kroger.com

The site for Kroger makes good use of alignment. The circle images are aligned both vertically and horizontally. The text below the images is also aligned vertically at the top and horizontally. This use of alignment gives the site a clean, organized look.

White Space

IKEA
www.ikea.com

The use of white space on the IKEA website gives the page a clean, uncluttered look. The space between elements, along with text alignment leadsthe eye to the call to action buttons and is very effective.

Contrast

T-mobile
www.t-mobile.com

On this site the use of a white font over a magenta background is striking. Another area of contrast is the magenta font on the white background. The usage of color and contrast promote brand recognition and lead the eye to the different plans offered.
